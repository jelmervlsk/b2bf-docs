## Core features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card icon="calendar" href="/core-features/sessions" title="Sessions & Scheduling">
    Create group classes, private sessions, and recurring schedules. Manage capacity and waitlists.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="ticket" href="/core-features/bookings" title="Bookings">
    Clients book online or you book them manually. Confirmations, cancellations, and waitlist promotions are automatic.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="users" href="/core-features/clients" title="Clients">
    Manage client profiles, view booking history, merge duplicates, and send invites.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="credit-card" href="/core-features/payments" title="Payments">
    Accept online payments via Stripe or let your customers pay at location (plan-dependent). Invoices, refunds, and receipts handled for you.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="user-group" href="/core-features/team" title="Team">
    Invite coaches and admins. Each role sees only what they need.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="globe" href="/core-features/public-portal" title="Client Portal">
    Your clients get a branded public page to browse, book, pay, and manage their own schedule.
  </Card>
</Columns>
