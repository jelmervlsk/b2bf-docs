Your location is the physical place where sessions take place. It drives your public booking page and everything clients see when they visit it — from your logo in the header to the color of each section.

Book2befit supports multiple locations on a single account, each with fully independent settings.

## Creating a location

Go to **Location** in the dashboard and click **+ Add Location**. Enter a name and timezone, then click **Create Location**. You're taken directly into the location's settings tabs.

## Location tabs

### General

Set the display name and timezone. The timezone controls how session times are displayed on the public booking page for clients in that area.

Toggle **Public page** ON when your studio is ready to take bookings. Toggle it OFF to hide the page during setup or temporary closures without deleting the location.

### Address

Enter your studio's address using the autocomplete search — fields populate automatically from the selection. The address appears on invoices and on the public booking page.

### Business Hours

Define when your studio is open each day of the week. Toggle individual days off for rest days or permanent closures.

### Closures

Add specific closure dates — public holidays, annual shutdowns, or one-off closures. Existing bookings on a closure date are not automatically cancelled.

<Tip>
  Add closures well in advance so clients aren't surprised by a locked booking page.
</Tip>

### Images

<Note>
  The Images tab is available on all plans.
</Note>

This is where you give your public booking page a professional, branded appearance. There are three distinct image slots:

| Field | Recommended size | Where it appears |
|---|---|---|
| **Logo** | 600 × 200 px | Displayed in the **header** of your public booking page — the most prominent brand element clients see on every page |
| **Cover Image** | 1920 × 600 px | Full-width banner in the **hero section**, below the header. Replaces the plain hero background color when set. |
| **Gallery** | Any — up to 10 images | Shown in the **gallery section** of your public page to showcase your studio space |

All images are resized automatically on upload. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP.

<Tip>
  Your logo is the single most impactful thing you can upload. It appears in the top navigation bar on every page of your public portal — classes, booking, account, products — giving clients a consistently branded experience throughout.
</Tip>

### Contact

Add public-facing contact details shown on your booking page:

- Phone number
- Email address
- Website URL
- Social media links (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Threads, TikTok)

### Branding

<Note>
  Branding color customisation requires the **Growth plan** or above. See [Plan Tiers](/reference/tiers).
</Note>

The Branding tab lets you set the background color and text color for each section of your public page — so the entire portal matches your studio's visual identity.

| Section | What it covers | Text color options |
|---|---|---|
| **Header** | Top navigation bar where the logo sits | Black or White |
| **Hero** | The banner area with your studio name (only when no cover image is set) | Black or White |
| **Body** | Main content area — schedule, session cards, services | Black or White |
| **Footer** | Bottom section of the page | Black or White |

Each section has an independent background color (chosen via color picker) and a text color toggle (Black or White). Leave a section empty to fall back to the default theme.

**Example:** A dark navy header with white text + a light grey body with black text gives a premium, high-contrast feel without a developer.

## Multiple locations

If you run more than one studio, add each as a separate location. Use the **location selector bar** at the top of the Location page to switch between them. Each location is fully independent:

- Its own public booking URL (`/[slug]`)
- Its own business hours, closures, and contact details
- Its own logo, cover image, and gallery
- Its own branding colors
- Its own spaces and equipment

## Deleting a location

Click **Delete Location** at the bottom of the General tab and confirm. Sessions and bookings linked to that location are retained in your history.

<Warning>
  Deleting a location removes it from the selector and makes its public page inaccessible. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>
