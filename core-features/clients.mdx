The **Clients** section gives you a full view of everyone who has booked with your studio — their contact details, booking history, assigned products, and notes.

## Adding a client

There are two ways to get a client into your system:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Invite by email">
    Go to **Clients** and click **+ Add Client**. Enter the client's email address. They'll receive an invitation email with a link to create their account on your public booking page.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Client self-registers">
    When a client books through your public page for the first time, they create their own account. They appear in your Clients list automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Client profile

Each client profile shows:

- **Contact details** — name, email, phone
- **Booking history** — all past and upcoming sessions
- **Products** — any memberships or packages assigned to them
- **Notes** — internal notes visible only to your team

### Assigning a product

From a client's profile, click **Assign Product**. Select a product and pricing tier. This is used for memberships, class packs, or recurring subscriptions.

<Note>
  Products and memberships require the **Growth plan** or above. See [Plan Tiers](/reference/tiers).
</Note>

## Merging duplicates

If a client appears twice (e.g. they used two different email addresses), you can merge them into a single profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the primary profile">
    Navigate to the client you want to keep as the primary record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Merge Client">
    Use the **Merge** option from the client's action menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the duplicate">
    Search for and select the duplicate profile. Booking history from both profiles is combined.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Merging is irreversible. The secondary profile is deleted after the merge.
</Warning>

## Filtering and export

Filter your client list by status (Active · Inactive) or search by name or email. Use **Export CSV** to download the full list.

<Accordion title="Can a client update their own details?">
  Yes — clients can update their name, email, and password from their account page at `app.book2befit.com/[slug]/account`.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I remove a client?">
  Client records are retained for billing and history purposes. You can mark them as inactive, which removes them from active lists without deleting their data.
</Accordion>
